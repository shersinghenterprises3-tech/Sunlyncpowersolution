Frequently Asked Questions

Difference between On-Grid & Off-Grid?

On-Grid connects to the grid (surplus can be exported). Off-Grid runs independently with batteries and does not feed the grid.

How long do solar panels last?

Panels typically last 20–25+ years. Inverters and batteries have shorter lifespans and may need replacement sooner.

Do you assist with subsidies & loans?

Yes — we guide customers through PM Surya Ghar Yojna paperwork and rooftop subsidy/loan applications.
